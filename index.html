our Signature Thekua cookies are handmade using a Traditional recipe passed down through generations. Made with whole wheat flour, sugar , ghee and a blend of cardamon and fennel seeds, these cookies offer a perfect balance of sweetness and texture.

key ingredients:

  • Stone-ground whole wheat flour
  • Organic jaggery (unrefined cane sugar)
  • Pure ghee (clarified butter)
  • freshly ground cardamon
  • toasted fennel seeds
  • coconut flakes
